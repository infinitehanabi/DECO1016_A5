Pets For Life Animal Shelter is always in need of donations.


  • $40.00 will feed one of our cats or kittens for a month.
  • $125.00 will pay for a vet to de-sex a cat or kitten at our shelter.
  • Any donation will be used it to help cover the costs of running the shelter and caring for our cats and kittens.

Your generosity will help us cover the costs of:

  • Insurance
  • Extensive veterinary expenses including, but not limited to, desexing, micro chipping and
  • vaccinating.
  • Food, bedding, parasite prevention, cat litter, toys, equipment and other ongoing feline
  • maintenance costs.
  • Advertising of cats and kittens for adoption on the radio and in newspapers.
